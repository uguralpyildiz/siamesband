THE BEGINNING

SIAMÉS was formed in 2013 in Buenos Aires by Barbie Williams and Walter Kebler, blending indie pop with electronic rock.

THE VISUAL IDENTITY

Their rise was fueled by animated music videos created with Rudo Company, turning each release into a cinematic universe.

GLOBAL BREAKTHROUGH

Tracks like “The Wolf” and “Mr. Fear” went viral on YouTube, reaching millions without traditional promotion.

PURELY INDEPENDENT

Without a major label, SIAMÉS built an international audience through digital platforms, visuals, and loyal fans.
